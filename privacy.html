Privacy Policy

SORUVALAB ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner. We adhere to professional global data protection standards to ensure your information is handled with the highest level of care across all our digital applications, cloud services, and simulation tools.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our services:

  • Contact & Account Information: Email address for unique account provisioning, authentication, service delivery, and direct technical support.
  • User-Provided Content & Telemetry: Prompts, queries, text inputs, or configuration data you supply while interacting with our digital tools and AI platforms.
  • Transaction & Billing Data: Payment history, subscription logs, and recurring transaction statuses. All data is processed securely through our designated third-party billing processor.
  • Technical & Log Data: Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser specifications, operating system information, access timestamps, and general usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is utilized strictly for the following operational purposes:

  • Provisioning, personalizing, and maintaining our AI-driven insights, generation engines, and cloud software.
  • Processing encrypted recurring billing transactions and verifying active subscription accounts.
  • Delivering customer support, handling technical inquiries, and debugging operational pipelines.
  • Monitoring infrastructure security, mitigating malicious misuse, and maintaining platform integrity.
  • Complying with necessary global legal frameworks and regulatory benchmarks.

AI Data Processing & Model Training

Our platforms utilize automated algorithms and artificial intelligence to compute user-requested workflows. We strictly do not use your personal inputs, proprietary code strings, or custom prompts to train foundational AI models. Most interactive data processing is entirely ephemeral and handled via secure, enterprise-grade cloud API nodes.

Third-Party Integrations & Infrastructure

We rely on trusted global service providers to scale our applications. These processors are contractually or policy-bound to enforce data protections that align with international privacy requirements:

  • Payment Gateway: All transactions are processed exclusively via Stripe. SORUVALAB never stores, holds, or has access to your raw credit card numbers or sensitive CVV codes.
  • Cloud & Inference Infrastructure: Data may pass through secure cloud infrastructure vendors and low-latency AI inference nodes (such as Vercel and API computing endpoints) to deliver your computed outputs immediately.

International Data Rights & Frameworks

We recognize and respect user data privacy rights globally. Our workflows are designed to align with major international data protection frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) within the EEA, the UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and other applicable US state privacy directives.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you retain the following programmatic rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a secure transmission of the personal data we maintain associated with your account.
  • Correction & Erasure: Request immediate modification or permanent purging of your account references from our systems.
  • Revocation of Consent: Opt-out or limit optional system logging and automated tracking where applicable.

To invoke these privileges, please submit an electronic request to our legal desk below. We process all validated compliance requests within the required timeline designated by the governing jurisdiction.

Data Retention & System Security

We retain your data only for the absolute duration necessary to manage your active subscriptions or to fulfill the functional criteria of our platform operations. We employ strict industry-standard transport layer encryption and technical safety protocols to shield internal assets from unauthorized exfiltration or access.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is built exclusively for internal professional use, developer integration, and strategic educational analysis. We do not knowingly harvest or solicit data from individuals under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy
