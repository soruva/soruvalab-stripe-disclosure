Privacy Policy

SoruvaLab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner. We strive to adhere to global data protection standards to ensure your information is handled with the highest level of care across all our digital products and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our services:

  • Contact Information: Email address for product delivery, authentication, and customer support.
  • User-Provided Content: Inputs, text, or data you provide while using our digital tools and AI services.
  • Transaction Data: Payment history and purchase records (processed securely via our third-party provider).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing and personalizing AI-driven insights, software, and digital products.
  • Processing secure payments and managing product licenses.
  • Delivering technical support and responding to user inquiries.
  • Maintaining system security and preventing fraudulent activity.
  • Complying with applicable international legal and regulatory obligations.

AI Processing & Data Handling

Our services utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) to generate outputs. We do not use your personal inputs to train foundational AI models unless explicitly stated for a specific personalized feature. Most data processing is ephemeral and used solely to generate the results you request.

Third-Party Services & Analytics

We use trusted third-party providers to operate our services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data in accordance with privacy regulations:

  • Payment Processing: All transactions are handled by Stripe. We do not store or have access to your full credit card details.
  • Analytics: We may use tools like Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns. Data collected through these tools is typically anonymized.

International Data Privacy & Your Rights

We respect your privacy rights regardless of your location. Our practices are designed to align with major international data protection frameworks, including the GDPR (EEA), UK GDPR, Australian Privacy Act, and various US State Privacy Laws (e.g., CCPA/CPRA).

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction & Deletion: Request the update or permanent removal of your personal data.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Opt-out of non-essential data collection (such as cookies).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the email provided below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Data Retention & Security

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal mandates. We implement industry-standard encryption and technical security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for professional and educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or global regulations. Your continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
