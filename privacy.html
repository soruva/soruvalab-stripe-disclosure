Privacy Policy

SoruvaLab ("we", "our", or "us") is a registered sole proprietorship (individual business operator) based in Japan. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner across all our digital products and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our services:

  • User-Provided Content: URLs, product descriptions, prompts, or other text inputs you supply while using our tools.
  • Transaction & Billing Data: Payment confirmation and transaction IDs. All payment data is processed securely by Stripe — we never store your credit card details.
  • Technical & Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, access timestamps, and general usage patterns for security and service improvement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Generating AI-powered feedback and results for your session.
  • Processing payments and verifying purchase status.
  • Responding to support requests and refund inquiries.
  • Maintaining platform security and integrity.
  • Complying with applicable legal requirements.

AI Data Processing & Model Training

Our platforms use AI to generate feedback and analysis based on your inputs. We do not use your submitted content to train AI models. Data processing is handled via secure third-party API providers.

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following trusted third-party providers to operate our services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. SoruvaLab never stores your raw credit card numbers. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Groq: AI inference provider. Submitted content is processed through Groq's API to generate results. Subject to Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database hosting. Session and transaction data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting and infrastructure provider. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Submitted URLs and descriptions may be retained in our database for up to 30 days for operational purposes (e.g., refund processing), after which they are deleted. We do not retain your data beyond what is necessary for service delivery.

International Data Rights & Frameworks

We respect user privacy rights globally. Our practices are designed to align with major international data protection frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction & Erasure: Request correction or deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Opt-out: Limit optional data collection where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
