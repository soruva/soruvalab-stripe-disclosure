Business Overview

We actively sell digital products, including unpublished mobile application source code, software projects built with Flutter, and AI infrastructure concepts such as Velior Trust.

How Transactions Work

  • One-time payment per product
  • Digital delivery after payment (within 1–3 business days)
  • No subscriptions or recurring charges
  • No physical goods or ongoing services

Each transaction represents a single sale and transfer of a digital product to one buyer.

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are final and non-refundable once delivery has been completed.

All products are intended for business or professional use.
