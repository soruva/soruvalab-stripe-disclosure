Privacy Policy

SoruvaLab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and secure manner. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information across our digital products and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our services:

  • Contact Information: Email address (for product delivery and support).
  • User-Provided Content: Inputs, text, or data you provide to our AI tools and applications.
  • Transaction Data: Records of purchases made through our secure payment processor.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing AI-driven insights, roadmaps, and digital products.
  • Processing secure payments via our third-party provider.

  • Analyzing usage trends to improve product performance and user experience.
  • Preventing fraudulent activity and ensuring system security.

AI Processing & Data Handling

Our services utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automated algorithms to process user inputs and generate outputs. We do not use your personal inputs to train foundational AI models unless explicitly stated or required for the specific functionality of a personalized tool. Most processing is ephemeral and designed to provide immediate results.

Third-Party Services & Analytics

We use trusted third-party services to operate efficiently:

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal requirements. We implement industry-standard technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

Regardless of your location, we respect your privacy rights. Depending on your jurisdiction (such as the EEA/GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for professional and educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13.
